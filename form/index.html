    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of items do you remove?

    We remove almost everything! This includes furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and general household junk. The only items we don't accept are hazardous materials like chemicals, paint, and certain types of batteries.

    Do you offer same-day service?

    Yes! We offer same-day junk removal service in Howard County and surrounding areas. Availability may vary based on schedule, so it's best to book as early as possible.

    How much does junk removal cost?

    Our pricing is based on the volume of junk removed. We provide free, no-obligation quotes before any work begins, so you'll know exactly what to expect. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Absolutely! Grime To Dime is fully licensed and insured for your protection and peace of mind. We maintain all necessary permits to operate in Howard County and surrounding areas.

    What happens to the items you remove?

    We're committed to eco-friendly disposal practices. We sort all items and recycle or donate as much as possible. Only items that cannot be recycled or donated are taken to approved disposal facilities.
