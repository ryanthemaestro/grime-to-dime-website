Partnership FAQ

Do you offer agent or portfolio pricing?

Yes — for recurring work or multi‑property portfolios, we can discuss preferred pricing based on volume and frequency.

How do you handle access if no one can meet onsite?

We can use lockboxes, codes, or coordinate with tenants/building staff. We’ll follow your instructions and confirm when we’re in and out.

Can you invoice an owner or property company directly?

Absolutely. We provide digital receipts and can invoice owners, PM companies, or your brokerage as requested.
