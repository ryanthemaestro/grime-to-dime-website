Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2025

Welcome to Grime To Dime ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website grimetodime.com or use our junk removal and hauling services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and address when you request a quote or contact us
  • Service location details and preferred service dates/times
  • Payment information when processing transactions
  • Communications and correspondence with our team

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, IP address, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, referring website)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our junk removal services
  • Process your service requests and payments
  • Communicate with you about services and updates
  • Send promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Analyze and improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and contractors who assist in our operations
  • Payment processors for secure transaction handling
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
